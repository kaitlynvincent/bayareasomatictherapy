Our mission

Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center exists because trauma recovery shouldn’t be a luxury. We serve people who can pay market rate and people who need affordable, consistent care — so more of us can heal in ways that are sustainable, relational, and real.

Interested in supporting the mission? Visit our About page or reach out — we welcome donors, community allies, and partners who care about access to trauma-informed therapy.
