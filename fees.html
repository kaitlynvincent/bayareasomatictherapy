Fees & accessibility

Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center is a nonprofit. Our fee structure is designed to support access: clients who are able to pay full fee help make low-fee trauma group therapy possible.

1:1 individual therapy

  • Full fee: $180/session
  • Sliding scale: $120–$180/session (based on income)
  • Consultation: free 15-minute call

Sliding scale spots are limited and offered based on availability.

Group therapy

  • Low-fee groups: $30/week
  • Format: 8-week cycles
  • Focus: CPTSD/trauma-informed nervous system support

Groups are intentionally affordable as part of our nonprofit mission.

How sliding scale works

  • We aim to choose a fee that is sustainable for you and for the program.
  • We may invite fee adjustments over time as circumstances shift.
  • Our priority is consistent care that supports long-term healing.
