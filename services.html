Sliding scale somatic therapy

Somatic therapy supports healing through the mind–body connection: noticing sensation, emotion, impulse, and the protective strategies your nervous system learned to survive.

What this can support

  • hypervigilance, anxiety, shutdown, overwhelm
  • relational trauma and attachment wounds
  • boundaries, voice, self-trust
  • shame and inner criticism
  • stress and chronic over-functioning

We work gently and collaboratively. You will never be pushed to share more than feels safe.

How we work

  • pacing, consent, and choice
  • grounding and regulation skills
  • resourcing and stabilization
  • tracking patterns with compassion
  • integrating insights into daily life
