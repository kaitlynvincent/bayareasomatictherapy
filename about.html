Why we exist

Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center was created to expand access to trauma-informed care in San Francisco and the Bay Area. We believe healing happens through safety, support, and connection — and that therapy should be available to people across income levels.

Our mission

To offer sliding scale somatic therapy and low-fee group therapy for CPTSD/trauma survivors, making trauma-informed care more accessible.

Our vision

A Bay Area where trauma survivors have consistent support, embodied tools, and community care — not just crisis response.

Our values

Safety • Choice • Consent • Dignity • Accessibility • Relational healing • Sustainability

How our nonprofit model works

We offer two pathways: individual therapy and low-fee group therapy. Clients who are able to pay full fee help subsidize low-fee groups, so more people can receive consistent, trauma-informed care.

Sliding scale 1:1 somatic therapy

  • Full fee: $180/session
  • Sliding scale: $120–$180/session based on income
  • Free 15-minute consultation

Low-fee trauma group therapy

  • $30/week
  • 8-week cycles
  • Structured, supportive, CPTSD/trauma-informed groups
