Affordable group therapy for CPTSD & trauma survivors

Our groups are designed for people who want trauma-informed support that is structured, steady, and accessible. We focus on nervous system skills, relational safety, and reducing isolation — without pressure or forced sharing.

Structured & supportive

Clear agreements, pacing, and a consistent group format to support safety.

Practical skills

Resourcing, grounding, boundaries, and regulation tools you can use in everyday life.

Healing in community

Connection that’s gentle and attuned — you don’t have to do this alone.

Meet your group facilitator

Group Facilitator • Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center

Our groups are facilitated by a somatic therapist who supports trauma survivors with a warm, grounded, nervous-system-informed approach. We believe group therapy can be deeply healing when it’s paced, structured, and rooted in choice.

When you join a group, we’ll share clear expectations, group agreements, and what to expect each week — so you can feel oriented, supported, and not alone.
